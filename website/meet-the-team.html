The people behind it

Meet the team

We are a group of young people united by one goal: to bring hope and support to children and families affected by cancer. Each of us takes on a role to keep Hope's Horizon running, but we all share the same passion for making a difference. 💕

🩷

Diya Rajkumar

Founder & President

Hi! I'm Diya Rajkumar, and I'm a freshman at the Charter School of Wilmington. I started Hope's Horizon because I wanted to find a way to support kids and families going through childhood cancer. At school, I'm involved in BPA, Key Club, Red Cross Club, UNICEF, CureQuest, and Debate, where I hold several leadership positions and enjoy taking on leadership roles whenever I can. I also volunteer at my local library and help with community events. Outside of school, I love listening to music, watching Formula 1, and spending time with my friends and family. I'm excited to help Hope's Horizon grow and make a difference in our community.

🧡
