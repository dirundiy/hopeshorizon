Our work

What we will do

Here's how we turn our mission into action. Every project is created and run by our team, and made possible by the support of people like you. 💪

✍️

Make cards

Handmade cards to bring a smile, comfort, and encouragement to children during treatment.

🎁

Make care packages

Thoughtfully prepared care kits filled with items that bring joy and hope throughout the cancer journey.

🎗️

Fundraise & donate

Raising funds to donate to cancer foundations, children's hospitals, and cancer institutions.

What's inside a care kit

  • 🧸 Cozy comfort items like soft toys and blankets.
  • 🎨 Activities and crafts to pass the time and spark joy.
  • 💌 A handwritten card to remind them they're not alone.
  • ✨ Little treats and goodies to brighten a tough day.

Want to help us make more? Every donation and volunteer makes a real difference. 💛
